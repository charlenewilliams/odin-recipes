The World's Best Shrimp Parmigiana Recipe



Home


This is the world's best shrimp parmigiana recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs.
Even if you are a complete beginner in the kitchen, you will find the instructions easy and fun.
So grab your apron and your ingredients and get ready to delight your family with this amazing recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups canola oil
  • 1 tbs paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp ground thyme
  • 1 tsp dried marjoram
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup Italian-style bread crumbs
  • 2 tbs grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs water
  • 1.5 lbs. shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 (32 oz.) jar roasted red pepper and garlic tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

    • Steps
    1. Pour oil into a pot and heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

    2. Combine paprika, salt, black pepper, oregano, thyme, marjoram, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne powder in a bowl. Mix 1 tablespoon of spice mixture with bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese in a separate bowl.
      Mix remaining spices with flour in a third bowl.

    3. Beat eggs and water together in a bowl.

    4. Dip each shrimp in flour mixture, then in egg mixture, and then in bread crumbs and place on a plate.

    5. Fry shrimp in hot oil until they float and turn golden brown, about 1 minute. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate.

    6. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.

    7. Pour tomato sauce into an 8-inch square casserole dish and top with fried shrimp, dipping them to coat with sauce. Top with mozzarella cheese.

    8. Broil in the preheated oven until cheese starts to brown, 3 to 4 minutes.

    9. Get your forks and enjoy!
