This is the world's best lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs.
Even if you are a complete beginner in the kitchen, you will find the instructions easy and fun.
So grab your apron and your ingredients and get ready to delight your family with this amazing recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef, chicken or turkey
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce and paste
  • Sugar
  • Italian seasoning, salt, pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella and Ricotta cheese
  • Eggs

    • Steps
    1. Cook the meat: Cook the meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products,
      half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.

    2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.

    3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.

    4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.

    5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.

    How Long to Cook Lasagna

    In an oven preheated to 375 degrees F, this homemade lasagna should be perfectly baked in about 50 minutes (30-40 minutes covered, 5-10 minutes uncovered).
